Instructions: First Puzzle-Solving Task

For the first task of this study you will solve a puzzle. You and your partner will work on solving the same puzzle independently.

You can earn real money by completing this task. If you correctly solve the puzzle, you will receive an extra $1.00 bonus payment. Similarly, if your partner correctly solves the puzzle, your partner will receive an extra $1.00 bonus payment. If you both correctly solve the puzzle, both of you will receive $1.00 bonus payments. In other words, your own earnings are in no way affected by your partner's earnings.

For the first puzzle task, one of you will be randomly selected to receive a hint that will make solving the puzzle easier. The other participant will be asked to complete the puzzle without receiving a hint. The participant that is to receive the hint will be randomly selected by the system.

Press "Next" when you're done reading the instructions and are ready to start the Puzzle-Solving Task.
